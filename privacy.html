PRIVACY POLICY

1. Data Collection & Architecture

At Forge Creative Labs, our primary directive is building high-performance systems. When you interact with our platform, initiate a project request, or collaborate on digital engineering, we collect only the essential data required to architect and deliver your project. This includes basic contact information, project parameters, and technical requirements provided strictly by you.

2. Usage of Telemetry Data

We may monitor anonymous, non-invasive traffic data specifically to measure the performance, framerate, and scalability of our portfolio module. This data is strictly utilized for engineering optimizations, zero-blur standard verification, and internal quality assurance. We do not engage in third-party data brokerage.

3. Security Infrastructure

All client communications, intellectual property, and project blueprints are treated with the highest degree of confidentiality. Project files and source code are maintained on encrypted, private repositories during the development cycle to prevent unauthorized access, data leaks, or corporate espionage.

4. Protocol Modifications

Forge Creative Labs reserves the right to dynamically patch and update this privacy policy as our services and technological capabilities scale. Any major architectural shifts in how we handle client data will be logged on this page.

5. Data Ownership Rights

You maintain full sovereignty over your personal data. Should you wish to sever all digital ties, request a data audit, or enforce the deletion of your historical project logs, contact us directly via X (Twitter) Direct Messages at @ForgeCreative26.
